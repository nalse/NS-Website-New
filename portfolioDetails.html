Eduventos - AI Supported Education Platform
Overview

Eduventos is an AI-powered education platform designed to make learning more personalized, efficient, and accessible. The product combines adaptive lessons, smart analytics, and an intuitive user experience to support students, teachers, and institutions. NS Studio stepped in to shape the platform’s UX, structure the product, and bring the vision to life with clarity and purpose.

The Goal

Create a personalized learning experience using AI-driven recommendations. Simplify the teaching workflow with clear dashboards and intuitive tools. Increase student engagement through adaptive lessons and progress tracking. Make complex educational data easy to understand for students, teachers, and admins. Support scalability so the platform can grow with new features and user groups. Design an interface that feels modern, clean, and frustration-free for all users.
The Solutions
We started by mapping out the real needs of students, educators, and institutions to ensure the platform felt intuitive, adaptive, and genuinely helpful.

01

In-depth UX research to understand user behavior, learning patterns, and common friction points in digital education tools.

02

Redefined the information architecture to support clear navigation, faster content discovery, and smoother task completion.

03

Implemented AI-driven features such as personalized learning paths, progress insights, and smart recommendations to elevate engagement.
